# Experiment 0023: Personality Stripped

## Results
Mean sim: 0.7906 (barely changed from 0.7972). Personality markers are in 121/251 entries.

## Analysis
Stripping "my skeptical nature", "given my deliberate approach", etc. makes almost no difference. The personality phrases contribute very little to the embedding similarity compared to the resource vocabulary and reasoning structure. This confirms that the dense ball is not caused by any single repeated phrase — it's the fundamental structure of the text.
