# Experiment 0042: Action Result as Outcome Signal

## Results
Classified action results: positive (38), weak (80), pending (22), neutral (61), info (40), social (10).

### Key findings
Situation 4 ("stable but water low"):
- Bake surplus: **56% positive** — best strategy by far
- Forage: **50% positive** — also works
- Offer trades: **0% positive** — just creates pending offers that go nowhere

Situation 3 ("critical risk, zero bread"):
- Bake/forage: **33% positive**
- Default forage: **5% positive** — Helen's default is her worst strategy
- Send DM: **8% positive** — mostly creates pending states

## Comparison with 0040 (consecutive entry outcome)
Both approaches converge: baking consistently outperforms foraging in crisis, and messaging/offering creates pending states that rarely resolve. The action result gives a more granular signal (positive vs weak vs pending) compared to the binary crisis_resolved.

## Key consolidation output
"When in crisis with zero bread, baking immediately produces resources 33% of the time vs foraging's 5%. Your default skeptical response (forage) is your least effective crisis strategy."

This is the kind of knowledge an agent could actually USE to make better decisions.
